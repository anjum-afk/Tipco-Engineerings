## Quick Reference — Top 5 Things to Know

1. **This is B2B industrial machinery** — buyers are factory managers and procurement teams, not casual browsers. They arrive with a product in mind. Get them to specs and an enquiry form fast.
2. **Wahal Engineers (wahalengineers.com) is the design benchmark** — cleanest competitor. Beat their product grid, match their navigation simplicity.
3. **Mecworks Innovation has the best product cards** — but ruins it with a popup on load. Copy the cards, skip the popup.
4. **No competitor has fast load time, spec sheets, or mobile-first design** — these three things alone would make Tipco the clear market leader.
5. **The redesign is React + Vite** — every design decision maps to a component. Design with that in mind.
