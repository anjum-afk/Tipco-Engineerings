## Financials (the real growth story)

| Metric | FY2024 | FY2025 | FY2026 |
|--------|:------:|:------:|:------:|
| Sales | ₹101 Cr | ₹133 Cr | ₹145 Cr |
| Net profit | ₹8 Cr | ₹16 Cr | ₹25 Cr |
| Operating margin | 12% | 18% | **26%** |

- **3-yr CAGR:** sales **59%**, profit **115%**. ROE **38%**, ROCE **36.2%**.
- **The standout: operating margin more than doubled (12% → 26%) in three years** — profit is growing far faster than revenue (FY26 sales +9% but profit +56%). Operational leverage / mix improvement is the core equity story.
- **Balance-sheet watch-outs:** borrowings ₹41 Cr; **debtor days 169**, **cash-conversion cycle ~210 days** — very working-capital-heavy (normal for project-based capital goods, but it's why IPO funds went to WC + debt).
- **Revenue per employee is high:** ~₹145 Cr across ~96 permanent employees ≈ ₹1.5 Cr/head.

## The "1985 / 1988 / 2021" timeline, untangled

- Business traces to **~1985–1988 as "Tipco Engineering Works"** (proprietorship/partnership) — still alive online as `tipco.co.in` and `indiamart.com/tipcoengineeringworks`.
- **Incorporated as Tipco Engineering India Pvt. Ltd. in Sept 2021**, converted to Limited, **IPO'd 2026.**
- So **"Since 1985" = legacy brand; 2021 = the listed corporate vehicle.** Both are true; the site should tell this story deliberately instead of contradicting itself (current pages cite 1985 / 2021 / "1980's" / ~2008 inconsistently — see [../tipco-legacy-website/15-trust-and-media.md](../tipco-legacy-website/15-trust-and-media.md)).

## Why this changes the redesign brief

1. **The site is now investor-facing.** The investor section must be real, current, and compliant — yet `/catalogue` throws **PHP errors** and `/certificates` renders **empty**. For a listed company, a broken/empty corporate site is a credibility and (arguably) disclosure liability, not just UX debt.
2. **They have a great story they currently don't tell.** ₹145 Cr revenue, 26% margins, 100%+ stock gain, ISO 9001:2015 + ISO 45001:2018, two CNC-equipped units, exports (China Coating Show 2023 & 2024) — but the site shows **no stats band, empty certifications, anonymous clients, no case studies.** The redesign's biggest content win is simply *surfacing what's already true.*
3. **The ISO disconnect is the sharpest irony:** the company is genuinely **ISO 9001:2015 & ISO 45001:2018 certified** (per IPO disclosures), yet the website's certifications page is blank. Fix this first — it's a free, high-impact credibility gain.
4. **Audience widened:** add investors/analysts and the financial press to the buyer/dealer personas — captured as Persona 6 in [02-buyer-personas.md](02-buyer-personas.md).
