## The 6 things to know at a glance

1. **Tipco is BSE-SME listed since Apr 2026** — revenue ₹145 Cr, stock ~doubled post-listing. See [04](04-business-and-ipo-profile.md).
2. **Primary end-market is paint & coatings** — a ~₹96,000 Cr Indian industry in a capacity boom. See [03](03-industry-analysis.md).
3. **~28 products, 0 working brochures, broken catalogue/certs pages.** See content inventory `12`–`15`.
4. **Closest rivals:** Wahal (best site), Mecworks; full list in [01](01-competitor-landscape.md).
5. **Buyers are owner-led (SMEs) or committee-led (large plants)** — relationship + specs + trust. See [02](02-buyer-personas.md).
6. **Biggest redesign win:** surface the true credibility story (financials, ISO certs, public-company status) the site currently hides.
