## Strategic read

- **Day-to-day competition = Tier 1/2 Indian firms.** Win on web presence (beat Wahal), per-product spec sheets (nobody offers them), and now **public-company credibility** (Tipco is the only BSE-listed name in this peer set — a real differentiator). See [04](04-business-and-ipo-profile.md).
- **Tier 3 = aspirational benchmark + export competition.**
- **White space no competitor fills:** sub-2s site, downloadable spec sheets, real industry landing pages, case studies, genuine mobile-first, product videos, WhatsApp-in-enquiry. (From the competitive audit.)
