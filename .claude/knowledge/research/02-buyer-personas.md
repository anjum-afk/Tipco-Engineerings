## How industrial capital equipment is actually bought in India (the evidence)
- **Owner-led in SMEs:** ~60% of MSME procurement decisions involve the Founder/Owner (46% owner, 13% partner, 21% purchase manager). For Tipco's smaller customers, **the owner often *is* the buyer.**
- **Committee-led for larger/new-plant purchases:** *technical* decision-makers (engineers, plant/production managers) evaluate specs & compatibility; *commercial* decision-makers (CFO, procurement, ops head) evaluate price, TCO, terms, supplier reliability.
- **Long, relationship-driven cycles:** ~4–8 weeks for standard equipment; 10–14 weeks for complex/regulated. Purchases are ₹10L–several Cr.
- **What moves them:** technical documentation, **specs & datasheets**, **case studies / references from similar plants**, ROI/TCO, supplier credibility (certs, track record), and fast human contact (call/WhatsApp). Discovery increasingly via Google + IndiaMART.

## Persona 1 — The Owner-Buyer (SME paint/ink/chemical manufacturer) ⭐ primary
**Who:** Proprietor / Director of a small-to-mid paint, ink, putty, or chemical unit (₹5–100 Cr turnover). 35–58. Tier-1/2 industrial hubs (Delhi-NCR, Gujarat, Maharashtra, South).
**Buying role:** Decides *and* pays. Pragmatic, cost-conscious, trust-driven.
**Trigger:** Expanding capacity, replacing an old mill, or starting a new product line.
**Journey:** Google / IndiaMART → 3–5 supplier sites + WhatsApp enquiries → price + delivery + "will they support me after" → site visit / demo → negotiate → buy.
**Needs from Tipco:** transparent specs, indicative pricing cues, **spare-parts & service assurance**, proof others like him bought (clients/case studies), trial run on lab machine, **instant WhatsApp**.
**Current site fails him:** 7.2s load, no spec sheets, anonymous clients, dead brochure buttons.

## Persona 2 — The Procurement / Purchase Manager (mid–large manufacturer)
**Who:** Purchase Head / VP Ops at an established paint/pharma/chemical maker. 38–52.
**Buying role:** Runs a committee buy; must *justify* vendor choice to management.
**Trigger:** Approved capex; needs ≥3 comparable quotes.
**Needs:** side-by-side model specs, **downloadable datasheets**, certifications (ISO), client references, GST-compliant formal quote, reliable lead time. Won't fill long forms — wants to call.
**Moves on:** credibility + documentation + TCO, not flash.

## Persona 3 — The Project / Plant Engineer at a new-capacity buyer 🚀 growth persona
**Who:** Project Engineer / Production Manager at a **large new entrant or expanding plant** (e.g., the paint-war capacity wave — see [03](03-industry-analysis.md)). 30–45.
**Buying role:** *Technical* gatekeeper specifying equipment for a new line; recommends, procurement contracts.
**Trigger:** New plant / production-line build-out.
**Needs:** **throughput & capacity data**, material-of-construction, integration into a full line, **turnkey "production line setup"** capability, references from comparable plants, vendor's ability to scale & support. High-value, multi-machine orders.
**Why now:** the single biggest growth opportunity for Tipco. The site should speak to him with line-setup content and engineering depth.

## Persona 4 — The R&D / Lab Technician
**Who:** R&D chemist / lab manager at pharma, cosmetics, food, coatings. 26–40.
**Buying role:** Influencer — selects lab-scale machines for pilot/formulation; manager approves.
**Needs:** detailed lab-machine specs (RPM, capacity, MOC), application suitability ("for pharma/cosmetics"), catalogue PDF, **WhatsApp for quick technical Qs**, dimension/detail images.
**Note:** entry point to a future production-scale sale — treat well.

## Persona 5 — The Export / International Buyer
**Who:** Overseas distributor or manufacturer (Middle East, Africa, SE Asia, China-region) met at trade shows (China Coating Show 2023/24).
**Buying role:** Distributor (resale) or end-user; price + quality + export support driven.
**Needs:** clear product range, export/shipping capability, international credibility (now: **public-company status + ISO certs**), English spec docs, responsive contact.
**Path:** add an export/partner track (Tipco already recruits dealers via `/partner`).

## Persona 6 — Investor / Analyst (post-IPO, secondary) 📈
**Who:** Retail/HNI investor, SME-IPO analyst, financial press — new since the Apr 2026 listing.
**Needs:** working investor section (announcements, results, shareholding), a credible corporate site that reflects a listed company. Currently underserved (broken catalogue, empty certs). See [04](04-business-and-ipo-profile.md).

## Page priority by persona

| Persona | Key journey |
|---------|-------------|
| Owner-Buyer (SME) ⭐ | Home → Product → specs/price cues → WhatsApp/Enquiry |
| Procurement Manager | Category → compare models → datasheet → certs/clients → formal quote |
| Project Engineer 🚀 | Industry/Production-line page → engineering depth → references → contact sales |
| R&D / Lab Tech | Product detail → specs → catalogue → WhatsApp |
| Export Buyer | Products → export/partner → credibility → contact |
| Investor | Investors section → announcements/results |

**The product detail page + industry/production-line pages serve the highest-value personas — design them first.** Across every persona the recurring unmet needs are the same four: **fast specs, downloadable datasheets, visible credibility (certs/clients/case studies), and one-tap human contact.**
